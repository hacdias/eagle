Protocol Labs (PL) is a research and development institution for improving the Internet technology. Focusing on IPFS, the InterPlanetary File System, we're building a decentralised, P2P and content-addressable network for future-proofing the web.

<!--more-->

In late **2017**, I was offered an Open Source Scholarship at Protocol Labs with the intent of revamping some of their GUI projects. After a year at PL, I wrote a [blog post][4] about my experience so far. In the meanwhile, I have been promoted to **Software Engineer**.

## Achievements

- Shipped the [Awesome IPFS][1] website, a compilation of the most fascinating projects, datasets, tools and other material built using IPFS technology.
- Lead maintainer of [IPFS Desktop][2], an Electron application with over 800 weekly users and the most widely used IPFS client.
- Shipped [IPFS Web UI][3], a React-based web application used as a front-end to the IPFS world.
