Just published two minimal packages to help building micropub applications!

The first one is called [micropub-parser](https://github.com/hacdias/micropub-parser) and it's basically a port from [@aaronpk's](https://aaronparecki.com/) [pk3-micropub](https://github.com/aaronpk/p3k-micropub) package to JavaScript.

The second one is a small IndieAuth [middleware](https://github.com/hacdias/indieauth-middleware) that can be easily plugged into an Express.js app!
